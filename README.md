# Career Guidance & Student Support Portal

A mobile app that helps students discover career paths, access learning resources, connect with mentors, and find scholarships — built in support of **SDG 4: Quality Education**.

## About

The Career Guidance & Student Support Portal gives students free access to career guidance, learning materials, mentorship, and funding opportunities, all in one place, with the goal of promoting inclusive and equitable education and lifelong learning.

## Features

- **Landing Page** — App introduction with quick access to Career Assessment, Career Recommendations, Learning Resources, Mentorship, Scholarships, and SDG Impact, plus Sign In / Create Account options.
- **Authentication** — Log In (email/password, "Forgot Password?", Google sign-in) and Create Account (full name, email, password, confirm password, Google sign-up).
- **Home Dashboard** — Personalized greeting, notifications, SDG 4 banner, quick-access grid, and a profile completion progress bar.
- **Career Assessment** — Multi-select questionnaire (e.g. Mathematics, Science, Commerce, Arts & Humanities, Technology, Other) to determine student interests.
- **Career Recommendations** — Personalized career matches (e.g. Software Developer, Data Analyst, Civil Engineer, Medical Doctor) with match strength (High/Medium) and details.
- **Learning Resources** — Searchable, filterable (All, Past Papers, Study Guides, Videos) library of downloadable resources such as past exam questions, study guides, and instructional videos.
- **Mentorship Platform** — Browse available mentors by role (Career Counselor, University Lecturer, Software Engineer, Medical Doctor) with availability status and direct messaging; separate tab for "My Mentors."
- **Scholarships & Opportunities** — Searchable, filterable (All, Scholarships, Internships, Training) listings with funding status and application deadlines.
- **SDG Impact** — Dashboard showing the platform's contribution to SDG 4, with stats on students registered, resources shared, mentorship sessions, and opportunities shared.
